## Start here

New to the app? Three pages get you playing music:

1. **[Introduction](/guide/introduction)** — what it is, and what makes it different from a search box.
2. **[Installation](/guide/installation)** — the `.exe`, the `.apk`, and the one Android update rule that will save your playlists.
3. **[Quick Start](/guide/quick-start)** — your first search, your first playlist, your first download.

Already running it? The good stuff lives in **[The Player](/guide/player)** and **[Equalizer](/guide/equalizer)** — those are the pages where the app stops feeling like a search box and starts feeling like a music player.

::: tip Something not working?
**[Troubleshooting](/reference/troubleshooting)** covers the handful of things that actually go wrong — a song that won't play, music stopping when you leave the app, and the SmartScreen warning on Windows.
:::
