# Fair Use

This app is **for educational and personal use only**.

It doesn't host, store or distribute any copyrighted content — it plays what the public sources already serve, from your own device.

## Support the artists

**Buy the music you love, and use official streaming services.**

Streaming revenue is how the people making the music you're listening to keep making it. This app is not a replacement for paying them.

## Privacy

There isn't much to say, which is the point:

- **No account** — nothing is collected because nothing is asked for.
- **No server** — your device talks to the sources directly.
- **No telemetry** — no usage data, no crash reporting, no analytics.
- **No sync** — your library is on your device and stays there.
