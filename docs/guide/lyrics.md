# Lyrics

Lyrics come from **[lrclib](https://lrclib.net)**, an open lyrics database.

## Synced lyrics

When a synced version exists, lyrics scroll **line by line, in time with the music**.

**Tap any line to jump to that moment in the song.** It's the fastest way to replay a verse — much quicker than hunting for the right spot on the scrubber.

## Plain lyrics

When only an unsynced version exists, you get the full text instead. No scrolling, no timing, but the words are there.

## When there are no lyrics at all

lrclib is community-contributed, so coverage is very good for popular music and thinner for remixes, live versions and independent uploads.

If a track shows no lyrics, it usually means nobody has submitted them for that exact recording yet — a different version of the same song may well have them.

## Where to find them

Expand the full player and open the lyrics panel.
